Privacy Policy

Parakletos Beta Program

Last Updated: January 2025
Privacy First: We process worship audio in real-time for translation only. No recordings are stored, and your content remains private and secure.

1. Introduction

Parakletos Ltd is committed to protecting the privacy and security of churches and their congregations. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you participate in our Beta Program.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Church Registration Information

  • Church name and denomination
  • Location (city and country)
  • Contact person details (name, role, email, phone)
  • Congregation size and technical setup information

2.2 Audio Data (Temporary Processing Only)

Important: Audio content is processed in real-time and is NOT recorded, stored, or retained after the service ends.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide real-time translation during worship services
  • Maintain and improve translation accuracy
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Improve our AI translation algorithms

4. Data Security

  • End-to-end encryption for all audio transmission
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • No permanent storage of worship content

5. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information we hold
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
